Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started. Don't see your question? Send me a message and I'll be happy to help.

How long does it take to build my website?

Most websites are completed within 1–2 weeks after I receive your content, images, and branding materials. Larger projects like ecommerce stores may take 2–3 weeks. The timeline depends on how quickly we can communicate during revisions.

What do I need to provide to get started?

After you purchase, you'll complete a project intake questionnaire covering your goals, content, logo, brand colors, and design preferences. If you need help with content, the Content Assistance add-on is available. The more detail you can share about your business, the better the result.

What platform do you build websites on?

It depends on your needs. Most business and service websites are built with clean HTML and CSS hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Ecommerce websites are typically built on Shopify. I'll recommend the best platform based on your goals and budget.

Will my website be mobile friendly?

Yes — every website I build is fully responsive and looks great on phones, tablets, and desktops. Mobile-friendly design is a standard part of every package, not an add-on.

Can I make changes to my website after launch?

Yes. After your site is live, you can reach out for updates and changes. Minor edits are handled on a case-by-case basis. If you need ongoing updates regularly, ask about maintenance options when we connect.

Do you offer payment plans?

Payment is processed upfront through Stripe when you select your package. If you have questions about pricing or need a custom quote, reach out through the contact form and we can talk through your options.

What if I need more pages than what's included?

Additional pages are available as an add-on for $100 per page with any package. You can select this option directly at checkout or contact me before purchasing to discuss your needs.
