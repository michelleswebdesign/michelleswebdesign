About Michelle's Web Design

Built With Purpose, Designed With Care

Michelle's Web Design was created to help small businesses, churches, ministries, and ecommerce brands build a professional online presence without the confusion and overwhelm that often comes with creating a website.

As a small business owner, I understand the importance of making a strong first impression. Your website should reflect the quality of your work, communicate your message clearly, and help visitors feel confident in choosing your business.

Every project is approached with attention to detail, thoughtful design, and a focus on creating a user experience that is both beautiful and functional. From simple landing pages to full business websites and online stores, my goal is to create websites that serve both you and your audience well.

Why I Do This

Websites Should Feel Approachable

I know building a website can feel overwhelming, especially when you are trying to run a business, serve your community, or launch something new. My goal is to make the process feel clear, manageable, and encouraging.

A website should not just sit online. It should help people understand who you are, what you offer, and why they can trust you.
